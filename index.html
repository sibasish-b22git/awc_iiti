About Us
At the Animal Welfare Cell, IIT Indore, we are dedicated to the care, protection, and well-being of the animals within our campus. Our mission is to ensure a safe, healthy, and compassionate environment for all animals, fostering a community that respects and values the lives of our furry friends.
We strive to promote animal welfare through:
  • Rescue and Rehabilitation:
    • Our team is committed to rescuing stray and injured animals, providing them with immediate medical attention, and facilitating their rehabilitation. We work tirelessly to ensure that every animal gets a second chance at life.
  • Sterilization and Vaccination:
    • To control the stray population and prevent the spread of diseases, we conduct regular sterilization and vaccination drives. These efforts are crucial in maintaining a healthy animal population on campus.
  • Feeding:
    • We ensure that stray animals on campus have access to regular, nutritious food through our designated feeding areas. This not only helps in their well-being but also keeps the campus clean and safe.
The Genesis of Our Journey
The Animal Welfare Cell at IIT Indore was born from a deep compassion for the campus's stray animals. Recognizing the need for dedicated care, a group of committed students and faculty members joined forces. Our mission has always been to ensure the well-being, protection, and health of these animals, creating a compassionate community that values every life. From humble beginnings, we've grown into a strong advocate for animal welfare on campus.
